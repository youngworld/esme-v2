A studio for original films & multimedia experiments inspired by the extraordinary ways of seeing everyday life.

In the flood of content online, is there such a thing as a fountain of youth?

Figuratively speaking, we mean the kind of flood that flows with "truths", right or wrong, that ultimately make us feel lost in society and more disillusioned than we ever expected. Drifting, we may stop growing. Alone, we choose to become an island. Happens to the best of us, doesn't it?

For esmé, we aim to make the kind of media that can show, quite literally, other ways of viewing the world, beginning with how we had more freely seen in our childhood or adolescence, and in spite of the number of our years aboard Spaceship Earth, stir us to utter still, "it feels good to be alive."

By means most honest, at times absurd, yet always cinematic, we're a Brooklyn-based filmmaking team that creates for digital canvases. Our commerical partners include Redscout, Google, Absolut, Montessori Model United Nations, & Vogue.com. We'd love to hear from you too.
